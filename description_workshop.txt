A teeny-tiny quality of life mod that adds a painting equivalent to wiring's Grand Design, the Mechanical Palette.

[h2] Usage [/h2]
The palette can be crafted with all three painting tools and some post-Skeletron materials.

Similarly to the Grand Design, players can right-click to open a menu where they can cycle through each of
the painting tools, or select one of the paints in their inventory to use.

One of the first 23 paints in inventory can be selected at anytime, and the Palette reaches about 16-blocks far.
